# Privacy Policy Draft

This draft is written for review before release. It is not legal advice and should be checked before public publication.

## Retirement Readiness Lab Privacy Policy

Effective date: To be added before release.

Retirement Readiness Lab is designed as a local-first retirement planning app. The MVP does not require an account, cloud sync, brokerage connection, bank connection, or analytics transmission of financial planning inputs.

## Information You Enter

The app may store retirement planning information that you enter, including:

- Current age and target retirement age.
- Filing status and mortality-table selection.
- Account balances.
- Spending estimates.
- Mortgage assumptions.
- Healthcare and Medicare assumptions.
- Social Security estimates and claiming age.
- Market, inflation, tax, Roth conversion, cash reserve, and long-term care assumptions.
- Scenario results and exported report content.

This information can be sensitive because it describes financial planning assumptions.

## How Information Is Stored

Scenario data is stored locally on your device. The app does not require a Retirement Readiness Lab account for the MVP.

If you uninstall the app, clear app storage, or use the in-app Delete Local Data control, local saved scenario data may be removed from the device.

## Sharing And Exports

The app creates exports only after you choose a share or backup action.

Exports may include sensitive financial planning assumptions. First-release export options may include:

- PDF reports.
- Plain-text reports.

JSON backup/import and CSV comparison exports are not visible in the first release.

Once you share or save an exported file outside the app, that file is controlled by the destination app, service, storage provider, or person you share it with.

## Network And Account Use

The MVP is intended to run without account creation, brokerage linking, bank linking, cloud sync, or financial-data analytics.

If future versions add cloud sync, analytics, account features, or external data integrations, this policy should be updated before those features are released.

## Financial Disclaimer

Retirement Readiness Lab provides educational estimates based on user-entered assumptions. It is not financial, tax, legal, or investment advice. Results are not guarantees and should be reviewed with qualified professionals before making retirement decisions.

## Data Deletion

You can use the in-app Delete Local Data control to remove saved scenarios from the device and reload sample plans.

You can also use Android system settings to clear app storage or uninstall the app.

## Children

Retirement Readiness Lab is not designed for children.
