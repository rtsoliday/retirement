# Icon Concepts

Ten launcher icon concepts were added as Android vector drawables under `android/app/src/main/res/drawable/`.

The active launcher icon currently uses the PNG artwork at `android/app/src/main/res/drawable-nodpi/retirement_readiness_icon.png`.

| # | File | Direction |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | `icon_concept_01_lab_flask.xml` | Lab flask with growth line; closest to the current product metaphor. |
| 2 | `icon_concept_02_shield_gauge.xml` | Shield and readiness gauge; emphasizes confidence and risk checks. |
| 3 | `icon_concept_03_horizon_path.xml` | Horizon path; more aspirational and retirement-focused. |
| 4 | `icon_concept_04_retirement_compass.xml` | Compass; emphasizes decision guidance. |
| 5 | `icon_concept_05_nest_egg_chart.xml` | Nest egg with chart; direct finance signal. |
| 6 | `icon_concept_06_summit_path.xml` | Summit path; goal-oriented and premium. |
| 7 | `icon_concept_07_calendar_percent.xml` | Calendar and percentage; emphasizes retirement timing. |
| 8 | `icon_concept_08_home_growth.xml` | Home and growth chart; household planning signal. |
| 9 | `icon_concept_09_lighthouse.xml` | Lighthouse; guidance and safety metaphor. |
| 10 | `icon_concept_10_vault_lab.xml` | Vault with lab mark; privacy and local-first planning. |
| 11 | `icon_concept_11_search_landscape.xml` | Magnifying glass over retirement landscape with growth bars; polished fintech planning signal. |

To test one as the active launcher icon, copy its visual paths into `ic_launcher_foreground.xml` or replace the foreground drawable referenced by `mipmap-anydpi-v26/ic_launcher.xml`.
